## Why it's in the library

- **Opening number masterclass.** "Tradition" doesn't just start the show — it *teaches the
  audience the rules* the rest of the plot will break, one daughter at a time. The contour shows
  every later rupture measured against that opening.
- **Bookend structure.** #1 builds the world; #14 dismantles it. Overlaying a user's contour
  against this reveals whether their own show closes the loop it opened.
- **Tension climbs across both acts** to Anatevka while energy stays moderate — a clean example
  of the energy/tension *divergence* the dual-contour view is built to show.
